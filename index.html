About us

First opening in the heart of Dublin City, we have always taken pride in bringing people fresh beautiful bouquets of flowers to a loved one or you simply want to brighten your own home with fresh scents and lively colour. Here at Blossoming Bláthanna we are passionate about the quality of our flowers, and our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. Your satisfaction is our number one priority, not just because it’s our job, but also because we genuinely care. We have a fantastic, comprehensive online flower service will help you in your busy life and bridge geographical distance, making it easy for you to make that connection with the special people in your life. We now have our Flower Subscription service up and running to have flowers delivered to your loved ones, even if it slips your mind.

Handcrafted floral bouquets for any occasion

Handcrafting each bouquet , they seal friendships and celebrate life’s milestones. Flowers lend comfort during times of loss and sadness, bring joy during the holidays and are a great way to say thank you. Flowers brighten homes, hotel spaces, restaurants and bars, office reception rooms and individual dining tables. They make a family dinner special and lend an air of festivity to simple gatherings. Whatever you wish to say, Blossoming Bláthanna can help you relay the message with a bouquet of freshly-picked blooms.

If you are in need of quick flower arrangements, Blooming Bláthanna will deliver quick, beautiful bouquets at a time of bereavement. Arrangements with a selection of crafted flowers with vibrant exotic blooms or a combination of delicate white and pale blooms.
